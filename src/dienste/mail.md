## MUA, MTA/MDA

* **M**ail **U**ser **A**gent (Client)
* **M**ail **T**ransfer **A**gent (SMTP-Server)
* **M**ail **D**elivery **A**gent (IMAP/POP3-Server)

## MX-Record

> Beispiel:
>
> \> dig MX afbb.de
>
> afbb.de.                86400   IN      MX      1 afbb-de.mail.protection.outlook.com.
